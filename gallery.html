Supporting Beams

Where heavy plant lifting equipment cannot be used traditional rope rigging is employed. This short clip shows the early stages of the build. All the beams are over engineered to provide more than adequate support to the treehouse above.

Roofing

Once the beams are installed, rafters and roofing materials are quickly laid. As with all building constructions, a vapour permeable membrane is used which allows the house to breath reducing condensation levels inside yet staying water tight.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Cladding

Timber is one of the most aesthetically pleasing of all cladding types. Commonly installed in long, narrow boards that can be fitted horizontally, vertically or diagonally, the outcome of timber cladding can be entirely customised to achieve the decorative finish you require.
