Inspirational Design and Exceptional Craftsmanship

We are a highly specialised and meticulous treehouse design and build company with a passion for the outdoors and environment. Using sustainable construction techniques our treehouses thoughtfully complement and blend in with the surroundings. We believe in delivering projects from initial concept to completion with minimal impact and disruption, giving as designed but better than imagined results.




What we do

Our treehouses are your dreams and working together we can design a bespoke structure which can be used for multiple purposes, from outdoor living, commercial (holiday rentals), work and play. The initial consultation will determine the ‘what for’ and ‘why’ and after a site visit to select a suitable location a design will be drafted and submitted for your approval. Much of the construction will be pre-fabricated off-site to minimise disruption and once we have agreed a suitable time we will complete the build at your property. We can also build elevated walkways, suspension bridges, Lapa buildings, Summer houses, covered platforms and zip lines. Get in touch so we can discuss your requirements.

Our Commitment

Our goal is to build you an outstanding treehouse in which to share and enjoy with family and friends. With sustainable construction techniques and beautiful materials we will endeavour to blend it seamlessly with the environment and create you dream outdoor space.
